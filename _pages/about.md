About me
======

Qian Zheng is a tenure-track professor (doctoral supervisor) under the "100 Talents Program" at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University. He works closely with [Prof. Gang Pan](https://person.zju.edu.cn/en/gpan) and [Prof. Huajin Tang](https://person.zju.edu.cn/en/0018196). His research primarily focuses on **brain-inspired computing** and **spiking neural networks**. He has published over 40 papers in top journals and conferences, including T-PAMI, CVPR, ICCV, NeurIPS, ICML, etc. He received the "Rising Star Award" from the Hangzhou Chapter of ACM in 2024. He serves as an editorial board member for international journals of [IEEE Transactions on Cognitive and Developmental Systems](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/xpl/RecentIssue.jsp?punumber=7274989) and [Neurocomputing](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/neurocomputing), and a reviewer for several top conferences or journals. 

Before joining Zhejiang University, he was a Research Fellow at [Rapid-Rich Object Search Lab](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/rose) (ROSE lab, directed by [Prof. Alex Kot](https://personal.ntu.edu.sg/eackot/index.html) and [Prof. Wen Gao](http://www.jdl.link/htm-gaowen/en_index.htm)), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, working closely with [Prof. Boxin Shi](https://camera.pku.edu.cn/) and [Prof. Xudong Jiang](https://personal.ntu.edu.sg/exdjiang/). His research at NTU is primarily on physics-inspired computer vision (reflectance modeling and its applications), neural rendering, and computational photography.
